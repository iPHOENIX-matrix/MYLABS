Kimetsu no Yaiba: Kyōdai no Kizuna

TANJIRO ON HIS PATH TO BECOME A DEMON SLAYER

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a manga series created by Koyoharu Gotoge,now published as JUMP COMICS imprints by SHUEISHA.

Since the release of the anime it has captivated the hearts and souls of fans worldwide with its tragic tale of humans versus demons, intense sword fighting, and sometimes even comical depictions of characters. the anime narrates the story of Tanjiro,a young boy and his resolve to save his sister. His entire family was wiped out by a demon and even worse his sister was turned into one .Speaking as an anime fan and an avid manga reader, this is gonna be one hell offer any anime series with its heart touching storyline intense fight sequences a wide range of impressive characters each standing out on its own and it's superb animation.

The film picks up the series after its first season which you can catch up on Netflix and believe me you won't regret it.The story picks up as our protagonist Tanjiro accompanied by his sister Nezuko and his friends Inosuke and Zenitsu board the Mn train which is set to be infested by demons. They’re joined by the flame Hashira Rengoku and together they fight against the demons of the train. I will not be giving too many spoilers here because I want to talk about demon slayer more in the future and that’s why I really want you guys to watch the first season and definitely this movie. If you want to have a glimpse of what you are going to expect then let me narrate to you one episode from the movie where our hero has to kill himself to awaken himself and his friends from a dead sleep.

RENGOKU WITH FIRE IN HIS HEART

I have fallen in love with the character of Rengoku, who is a kind man with fire running in his heart and there is a surprise for you guys in the end which really touched my soul.With the second season coming up in the coming months, you really need to catch up with this anime if you are an true otaku and even if you aren’t one , believe me you're going to enjoy it till the end.Let me know in the comments what are your thoughts about the anime.
