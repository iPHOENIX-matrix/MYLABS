GREAT STORIES FOR CHILDREN : A RUSKIN BOND CLASSIC

‘We grow old .But sometimes ,we hanker old friendships ,the old loves.Sometimes I wish I was young again….’

I picked this book up from my sister’s desk and when I saw that it’s been authored by Ruskin Bond and believe me when I say it’s a joy-ride for any age group especially for children. It’s surely a book for children and I would suggest all young readers who ar looking to start their reading journey or if they want ot slowly develop their vocabulary to go for this book.For older people my message would be that it’s going to take you back to your childhood and who else than Ruskin Bond to take you down the memory lane.

Talking about the content of the book it’s a beautiful collection of short stories all written and curated by Ruskin Bond.It’s set in Bond’s favourite hills which will transfer you back to his extraordinary world of stories .You will be introduced to an array of characters all unique in their own aspect and you are bound to celebrate each and every one of them.From the tales of a haunted hill to that of a special tree that deepens the bond between two generations you can just think what awaits you.There’s this curious case of a grandfather who keeps pythons as pets and of a selfish thief turning a leaf .

The best part of Ruskin Bond’s stories is that it has a lesson to teach but in a very simple manner.This is so important for kids at such an young age. If they can learn life lessons from stories without any pressure or tension it really helps them to grow up in a peaceful manner.
