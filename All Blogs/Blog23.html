LIFE’S COLORS SHALL NEVER ABANDON YOU

“I have been feeling kind of blue.”

Blue relaxes ,blue heals .

Blue is the serenity in pain – origin of undying passion.

Often when we talk about the sky and the ocean, the color blue comes to our mind.The color blue calms the mind ,brings balance and rest to the body .So we can say that blue as a color spotted anywhere in this world has a positive effect on us.

As human beings we desire loyalty and blue in all its sense is the embodiment of loyalty in human beings and dark blue signifies knowledge and power.

We did talk about blue but lets start from the beginning. Colors play a beautifully role in our life if we can embrace them in their originality. Best thing about life’s colors are that if you surround yourself with the right colors it’s going to have a very positive impact on your lifestyle . Our world is adorned with various colors and if you give it a thought we have never been without them – even a life of Black and White did bring a smile on the faces of thousands . The most fascinating thing lies in the fact that every individual sees different colors with a different perspective and in a different light .Similarly every single color has some different message ; some different emotion ; some different tranquility to communicate to different individuals.

Green maintains balance – bears the fruit of rebirth .

“When you’re green, your growing.”

The color green has immense healing powers and is always associated in rediscovering a new you,a reborn you.Green is the color of fertility ,the color of hope and the undying color of energy .We find the color green very relaxing as our eyes have been accustomed in seeing it around us since our birth . Green is the color of the youth and rejuvenates that fire in our souls to remove depression and anxiety.

Red is in my blood,I am red - I am the essense of fire,

The radiance of romance,
"Red is the ultimate cure for sadness.""; And my personal favourite .

Red is that color that heightens your emotions, strength, and power. It’s immensely intimidating and believe me guys , it’s never boring.The color red lets you be on the move anytime and every individual feels very strongly about it.

Red is the symbolic representation of both romantic love and its physical passion. We know Red Roses are a physical means of showing our affection and it also pumps the passion in our blood system. The color red motivates us and makes us feel confident bringing out the best in us .

This brings us to the end of our special segment where we talked about the essence of colors in our life .I wish to continue this never ending list of beautiful colors in another blog but unfortunately I have to take your leave now and do let me know how you liked this one !
