ETERNAL SUNSHINE OF THE SPOTLESS MIND : A BEAURIFUL TALE

Is this a romantic movie ? Yes and No. This movie might help you believe in love once again or might convince you to forsake the concept entirety and forever. But this must not stop you from watching this absolute gem of a movie because at the end of the day, this is reel and you should always approach one with an open outlook.

Directed by Michel Gondry ,the film is oriented around a couple who are going to take you for a rollercoaster ride until the end of the film.The film with an IMDb rating of 8.3 was released in 2004.

After a painful breakup ,Clementine (played beautifully by Kate Winslet ) decides to undergo a procedure to erase all memories of her past relationship with her boyfriend Joel (played by the evergreen Jim Carrey to perfection ). When Joel realizes how far Clementine is ready to go in order to forget him and the bond that they shared ,he takes the painful decision of going through the same procedure to forget the girl he loved .

The film has stunning visuals and a beautiful story . Its fast paced and will leave you entertained throughout . I don’t want to give up any spoilers as to how Joel searches for his long lost love but I will tell you one thing : this film will make you feel the pain of loss , the happiness of togetherness in good and bad times alike and to hold on to one another even when the odds seem to be totally against you.

The acting is top notch with the likes of Mark Ruffalo, Kirsten Dunst and Elijah Wood joining the star studded cast. Every character has an important role to play and to add to the missing pieces of the puzzle. The film might get confusing and complicated at parts but once you watch till the end of the film ,everything shall fall in place .

The film’s ending is one of the most iconic endings of all time and my favorite . Both Joel and Clementine had an ideal image of each other ,something all partners desire – something that is never ideally possible because everyone has their “sunshine” and their flaws. Both the characters grew through the film and so did us the audience. The ending is a beautiful reminder for all relationships which has its own ups and downs and to the concept of love. I would personally recommend everyone to watch this gem of a movie .
